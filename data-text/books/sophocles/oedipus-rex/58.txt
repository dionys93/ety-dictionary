OEDIPUS

You mean to say you got me from someone else? 
It wasn’t you who stumbled on me?


MESSENGER

No, it wasn’t me.
Another shepherd gave you to me.


OEDIPUS

Who? Who was he? Do you know? 
Can you tell me any details, 
things you are quite sure of?
 

MESSENGER

Well, I think he was one of Laius’ servants—
that’s what people said.


OEDIPUS

You mean king Laius,
the one who ruled this country years ago?


MESSENGER

That’s right. He was one of the king’s shepherds.


OEDIPUS

Is he still alive? Can I still see him?


MESSENGER

You people live here. You’d best answer that.
