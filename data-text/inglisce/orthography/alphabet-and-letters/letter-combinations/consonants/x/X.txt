X

PRONUNCIATION
The letter X represents one sound:
• /ks/ — as in "tax"
• /kʃ/ — as in "sexual"


EXAMPLES

    /ks/
    Mexico, flex, ax, tax, sax, flax
    to execute → to exequte
    to relax → to relaxe
    relaxation → relaxâcion

    /kʃ/
    sexual → sexeual
    contextual → contexeual
    luxury → luxeurie
    luxurious → luxeurieus
