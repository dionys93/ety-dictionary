R

PRONUNCIATION
The letter R represents one sound:
• /ɹ/ — as in "red"

EXAMPLES
    bar, car, far, hard, lard, Mars
    star → starre
    park → parc
    guard → gard

SPECIAL PATTERN
Sometimes R is preceded by silent H:
    reed → rhide
    rhythm → rhỳþm
    rhyme

TEACHING NOTES
R = /ɹ/ always. Initial RH- is a spelling variant with silent H.
