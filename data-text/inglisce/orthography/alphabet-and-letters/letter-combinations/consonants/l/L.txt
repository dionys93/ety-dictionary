L

PRONUNCIATION
The letter L represents two sounds:
• /l/ — light L (before vowels)
• /ɫ/ — dark L (before consonants or word-final)

EXAMPLES
    ball, ballad
    to lose → ta louse
    to look → ta luke
    to linger → ta lingre
    lamb → lam
    to fall → ta falle

TEACHING NOTES
L has two allophones (/l/ and /ɫ/) depending on position, but both are written as L.
