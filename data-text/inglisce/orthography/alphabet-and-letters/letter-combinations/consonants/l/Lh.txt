LH

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph LH represents one sound:
• /lj/ — L followed by a yod

RULES

1. Basic LH = /lj/
In most contexts, LH represents /lj/.

Examples:
    billion → bilhon
    million → milhon
    rebellion → rebelhon
    chameleon → camielhon
    Italian → Italhan

2. LH → LL Before U
Before U, /lj/ is written as LL instead of LH.

Examples:
    value → vallue
    to evaluate → ta evalluete

TEACHING NOTES
• LH = /lj/ (L + yod)
• Use LL instead of LH before U
