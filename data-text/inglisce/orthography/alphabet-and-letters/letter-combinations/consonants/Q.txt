Q

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Q represents three sounds:
• /k/ — after vowels (vowels become long, except A which is /æ/)
• /kw/ — before U + another vowel
• /kj/ — before U-E or Ú

RULES

1. Vowel + Q = /k/
After vowels, Q is pronounced /k/. The preceding vowel is lengthened.

Examples:
    to puke → to puque
    to joke → to joque
    parked → parqued
