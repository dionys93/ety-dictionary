M

PRONUNCIATION
The letter M represents one sound:
• /m/ — as in "mom"

EXAMPLES
    mom, mob, mop, mit, man, moan, moat, map
    to admit → to admitte
    to skim → to squime
    to scam → to scame
    to scram → to scrame
    hammer → hamor

M̃

PRONUNCIATION
The letter M̃ represents one sound:
• /mj/ — M followed by a yod

EXAMPLES
    ammunition → am̃onicion
    to amuse → to am̃use
    museum → m̃usíom
    music → m̃usic
    amulet → am̃olet
