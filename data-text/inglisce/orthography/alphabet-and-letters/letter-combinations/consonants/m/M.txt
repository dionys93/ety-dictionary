M

PRONUNCIATION
The letter M represents one sound:
• /m/ — as in "mom"

EXAMPLES
    mom, mob, mop, mit, man, moan, moat, map
    to admit → ta admitte
    to skim → ta skimme
    to scam → ta scame
    to scram → ta scrame
    hammer → hamor

TEACHING NOTES
M consistently represents /m/ in all positions.
