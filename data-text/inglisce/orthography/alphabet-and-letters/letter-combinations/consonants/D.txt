D

PRONUNCIATION
The letter D represents one sound:
• /d/ — as in "dog"

EXAMPLES
    donut, dog, dad

TEACHING NOTES
D consistently represents /d/ in all positions.


D̦

PRONUNCIATION
The letter ḑ represents one sound:
• /d͡ʒ/ — as in "judge"

EXAMPLES
    procedure → procíḑure
    to educate → to eḑucait
    education → eḑucâcion

TEACHING NOTES
ḑ = /d͡ʒ/. Used where Modern English has "du" pronounced as /d͡ʒu/.
