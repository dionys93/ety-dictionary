Z

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Z represents one sound:
• /z/ — as in "zoo"
/ʒ/ between vowels and before i or e and another vowel

OCCURRENCE
Less common than S for the /z/ sound.

EXAMPLES:

    /z/
    frieze
    zoo → zou
    zoology → zuólogie

    /ʒ/
    azure → azeure

TEACHING NOTES
Z = /z/ always. Often appears in words of foreign origin.
