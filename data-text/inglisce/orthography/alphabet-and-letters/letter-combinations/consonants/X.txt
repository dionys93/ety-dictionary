X

PRONUNCIATION
The letter X represents one sound:
• /ks/ — as in "tax"
• /kʃ/ — as in "sexual"

x̃ /kʃ/ in multisyllable words
/tʃ/ in monosyllable words or words derived therefrom


EXAMPLES

    /ks/
    Mexico, flex, ax, tax, sax, flax
    to relax → to relaxe
    relaxation → relaxâcion

    /kʃ/
    sexual → sex̃ual
    contextual → contex̃ual
    luxury → lux̃urie
    luxurious → lux̃urieus

    /tʃ/
    witch → uix̃e
    witchery → uix̃erie
