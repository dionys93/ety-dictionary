ţ

PRONUNCIATION
The letter ȶ represents one sound:
• /t͡ʃ/ — as in "nature"

EXAMPLES
    feature → fieţure
    mature → maţûre
    pasture → pasţure
    stature → staţure
    nature → nâţure
    natural → naţural

VARIATION
Sometimes /t͡ʃ/ is represented with TI:
    creature → crietior
    amateur → amatior
    question

TEACHING NOTES
ţ = /t͡ʃ/. Replaces -ture endings.
