L

PRONUNCIATION
The letter L represents three sounds:
• /l/ — light L (before vowels)
• /ɫ/ — dark L (before consonants or word-final)
/lʲ/ - palatalized l

EXAMPLES

    to look → to lôcue
    to linger → to lingre
    lamb → lam
    to fall → to falle
