C̃

PRONUNCIATION
The letter C̃ represents one sound:
• /kj/ — as in "cute"

POSITION
C̃ usually appears before the letter U.

EXAMPLES
    cute → c̃ute
    to evacuate → ta evac̃uete
    vacuum → vac̃ume
    cumin → c̃umin
    vacuous → vac̃uoʃ

TEACHING NOTES
C̃ = /kj/ (like "cue"). Almost always followed by U.
