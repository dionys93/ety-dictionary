IPA /ɛr/ — "SQUARE" VOWEL

SOUND OVERVIEW
- IPA symbol: /ɛr/
- Description: Mid-front unrounded vowel + rhotacization
- Audio equivalent: as in "square", "care", "bear"
- Frequency: Common in Inglisce, appears in verbs and derivatives

SPELLING PATTERNS

-ÂRE
