# IPA /ɑ/ — "FATHER" VOWEL

## Sound Overview

| Feature | Value |
|----------|--------|
| IPA | /ɑ/ |
| Description | Low-back unrounded vowel |
| Example in Modern English | father, lock, not |
| Frequency | Common; especially before consonant clusters |

---

## Spelling Patterns

| Category | Spellings |
|-----------|------------|
| Primary | O (before two consonants) |
| Stress-marked | Á (basic words with irregular stress) |

'*' Represents no change in spelling between Modern (Websterian) English and Inglisce
