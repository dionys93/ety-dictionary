# IPA /ɔɪ/ — "BOY" VOWEL

## Sound Overview

| Feature | Value |
|----------|--------|
| IPA | /ɔɪ/ |
| Description | Mid-back to high-front closing diphthong |
| Example in Modern English | boy, coin, choice |
| Frequency | Moderately common; more limited than /eɪ/ |

---

## Spelling Patterns

| Category | Spellings |
|-----------|------------|
| Primary | OI, OIE |

## Core Distribution Rules

### 1. -OIE (Verb-Final)

Verbs ending in Modern English **-oy** typically appear as **-oie**.

| Modern | Inglisce |
|--------|----------|
| to enjoy | to enjoie |
| to employ | to emploie |
| to destroy | to destroie |
| to boil | to boile |

#### Verb Paradigm Examples

| Base | 3rd sg | Past | Gerund |
|-------|----------|--------|------------|
| to enjoie | enjoies | enjoied | enjoying |
| to emploie | emploies | emploied | employing |
| to destroie | destroies | destroied | destroying |

---

### 2. OI (Medial Nouns)

Common nouns retain **oi** internally.

| Modern | Inglisce |
|--------|----------|
| coin, coins | coine, coins |
| choice | c̃oice |
| voice | * |
| noise | * |
| poise | * |

---

### 3. -OIE / -OYS (Simple Noun Pattern)

Some nouns use **-oie** in the singular and **-oys** in the plural.

| Modern | Inglisce |
|---------|-----------|
| boy, boys | boie, boys |
| toy, toys | toie, toys |
| joy, joys | joie, joys |
| decoy, decoys | dícoie, dícoys |

Related forms:

| Category | Inglisce |
|------------|------------|
| Adjective | soy, àloy |
| Interjection | ahoy |

---

### 4. Derived Nouns (-OIMENT)

Verbs in **-oie** often form nouns in **-oiment**.

| Modern | Inglisce |
|----------|-----------|
| enjoyment | enjoiment |
| employment | emploiment |

---

### 5. -OILE Variant Pattern

A related spelling appears with **-oile**.

| Modern | Inglisce |
|----------|-----------|
| to spoil | to spoile |
| spoil, spoils | spoile, spoiels |

#### Verb Forms

| Base | 3rd sg | Past | Gerund |
|-------|----------|--------|------------|
| to spoile | spoiles | spoiled | spoieling |
