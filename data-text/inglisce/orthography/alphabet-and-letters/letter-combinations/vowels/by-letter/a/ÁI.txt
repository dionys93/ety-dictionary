ÁI

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph ÁI represents one sound:
• /aɪ/ — as in "aisle"

OCCURRENCE
This letter combination is rare in Inglish.

EXAMPLE
    aisle → áile /ˈaɪəl/
    night → náit /naɪt/

TEACHING NOTES
ÁI is one of the rarest combinations in Inglish. It marks stressed /aɪ/ in exceptional words.
