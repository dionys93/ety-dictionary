AO

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph AO represents one sound:
• /aʊ/ — as in "sound"

OCCURRENCE
This letter combination is fairly common in Inglish.

EXAMPLES
    sound → saond /saʊnd/
    crown → craon /kraʊn/
    to found → ta faonde /tə faʊnd/
    foundation → faondâcion /faʊnˈdeɪʃən/

TEACHING NOTES
AO consistently represents the /aʊ/ sound. It appears in both verb and noun forms.
