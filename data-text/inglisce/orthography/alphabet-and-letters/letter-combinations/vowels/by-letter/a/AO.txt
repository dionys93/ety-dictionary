AO

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph AO represents one sound:
• /ɔ/ — as in "dawn"

OCCURRENCE
This letter combination is fairly common in Inglisce. Directly borrowed from French to represent /ɔ/.

EXAMPLES
    dawn → daone /dɔn/
    fawn → faone /fɔn/
    lawn → laone /lɔn/
    pawn → paone /pɔn/

TEACHING NOTES
AO consistently represents the /ɔ/ sound. It appears in both verb and noun forms.
