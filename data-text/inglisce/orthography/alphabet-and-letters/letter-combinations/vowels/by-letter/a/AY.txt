AY

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph AY represents one sound:
• /eɪ/ — as in "day"

RULES

1. Word-Final Position
AY typically appears at the end of adjectives, noun plurals, and in gerunds.

    gray
    bay, bays -> baie, bays
    day, days -> daie, days 
    ray, rays -> raie, rays
    play, plays -> plaie, plays
    way, ways -> uaie, uays

    to pay -> to paie, paying
    to stay -> to staie, staying
    to pray -> to praie, praying
    to delay -> to delaie, delaying
