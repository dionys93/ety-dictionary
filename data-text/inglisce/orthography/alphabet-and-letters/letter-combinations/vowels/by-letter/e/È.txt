È

PRONUNCIATION
The letter È represents one sound:
• /ɛ/ — as in "bed" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
È marks stressed /ɛ/ in words where stress placement needs clarification.

EXAMPLES
    the anemone → þi anèmonie
    the regrets → þa regrèts
    the cigarettes -> þa cigarèts
    infective -> infèctif
    defective -> defèctif



TEACHING NOTES
È = stressed /ɛ/. Used when regular E might not clearly show stress placement.
