# EU

The digraph EU represents three vowel sounds in Inglisce.

### PRONUNCIATION SUMMARY

- **EU** — /ju/ "few" — few → feue

— /u/ "rule" — rule → reule  

— /ə/ "luck" — luck → leuc

### The Digraph EU

#### Basic EU = /ju/
In most contexts, EU is pronounced /ju/.

**Examples:**  
few → feue /fju/  
view → veue /vju/  
eunuch → eunoc /ˈjunək/

#### EU = /u/ After Consonants
After certain consonants (especially R and TR), EU represents /u/.

**Examples:**  
rule → reule /rul/  
true → treu /tru/  
truth → treuþ /truθ/

#### EU = /ə/
EU represents /ə/ in specific vocabulary items.

**Examples:**   
buck → beuc /bək/  
duck → deuc /dək/  
luck → leuc /lək/

### Special Patterns

#### S + EU = /ʃ/
The letter S becomes /ʃ/ when followed by EU.

**Example:**  
shoe → seue /ʃu/
