Ê

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ê represents one sound:
• /ər/ — as in "earn" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ê marks stressed /ər/ in words.

EXAMPLES
    to emerge → to emêge
    the emergency → þi emêgencie
    to earn → to êne
    to yearn → to yêne
    to concern → to concêne
    the concern, the concerns → þa concêne, þa concêns

TEACHING NOTES
Ê = stressed /ər/. It shows both the R-colored vowel and stress placement.
