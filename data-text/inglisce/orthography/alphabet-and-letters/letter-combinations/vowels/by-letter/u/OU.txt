OU

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph OU represents one sound:
• /u/ — as in "moon"

EXAMPLES
    route, soup
    fool → foul
    stool → stoul
    boom → boum
    room → roum
    soon → soun
    moon → moun
    loon → loun
    coop → coup
    stoop → stoup
    loot → lout
    tomb → toum

TEACHING NOTES
OU consistently represents /u/. It replaces English "oo" when pronounced /u/.
