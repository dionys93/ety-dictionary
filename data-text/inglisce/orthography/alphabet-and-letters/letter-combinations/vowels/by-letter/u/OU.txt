OÛ

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph OÛ represents one sound:
• /ʊ/ — in modal verbs

RULES

Modal Verbs = /ʊ/

Examples:
    could → coûd
    would → oûd

TEACHING NOTES
• Modal verbs use OÛ for /ʊ/
• Always indicates stress
