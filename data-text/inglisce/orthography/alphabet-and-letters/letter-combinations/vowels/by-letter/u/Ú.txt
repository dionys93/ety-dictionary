Ú

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ú represents one sound:
• /u/ — as in "suit" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ú shows where the stress lies in the word.

EXAMPLES

    fruit, fruits → frute, frúts
    root, roots → rute, rúts
    suit, suits → sute, súts     
    recruit, recruits → recreute, recreuts


TEACHING NOTES
Ú = stressed /u/. Note how it often appears in plural forms.
