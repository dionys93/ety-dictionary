Ú

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ú represents one sound:
• /ju/ — as in "muse" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ú shows where the stress lies in the word.

EXAMPLES
    
    root → rute /u/
    roots → rúts /u/
    
    suit → sute /u/
    suits → súts /u/
    
    recruit → recreute
    recruits → recreuts

TEACHING NOTES
Ú = stressed /u/. Note how it often appears in plural forms.
