Ó

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ó represents one sound:
• /ɑ/ — as in "father" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ó marks stressed /ɑ/ in words where stress placement needs clarification.

EXAMPLES
    anonymous → anónimus
    phonology → fonólogie
    phonological → fonológical
    contract -> cóntract

TEACHING NOTES
Ó = stressed /ɑ/. Common in Greek-derived words with -ology.
