OI

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph OI represents one sound:
• /ɔɪ/ — as in "coin"

RULES

1. Verbs with OI
OI appears in verb forms ending in -OIE.

Examples:
    to enjoy → to enjoie
    to employ → to emploie
    to destroy → to destroie
    to boil → to boile

2. Nouns with OI
OI appears in nouns, including those derived from -OIE verbs.

Examples:
    enjoyment → enjoiment
    employment → emploiment
    the spoils → þe spoiels
    coin, voice

TEACHING NOTES
OI = /ɔɪ/ in all positions. Verbs typically end in -OIE while related nouns use -OIMENT.
