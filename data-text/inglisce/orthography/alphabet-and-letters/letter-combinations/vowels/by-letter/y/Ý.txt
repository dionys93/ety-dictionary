Ý

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ý represents one sound:
• /i/ — as in "see" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ý marks stressed /i/ in words where Y represents this sound.

EXAMPLES
    eel → ýle
    seal → sýle
    agreeing → agrýing

TEACHING NOTES
Ý = stressed /i/. Used when Y has the /i/ sound and needs stress marking.
