The Grave Accent in Inglish

In Inglish, the grave accent is used on the following vowels: à, ì, è, ò, ù, ỳ.

The grave accent indicates both pronunciation and stress in the word.

IPA Pronunciations of Accented Vowels:

    Àà: /æ/
    Ìì, Ỳỳ: /ɪ/
    Èè: /ɛ/
    Òò: /ɔ/
    Ùù: /ə/

Examples of Words Transcribed from English to Inglish:

    analysis → anàlysiʃ
    committee → comìti
    regret → regrèt
    anemone → anèmoni
    cost → còst
    lost → lòst
    Austria → Òstria
    disruptive → disrùptif
    reluctant → relùctant
    abundant → abùndant
    analytical → analỳtical
