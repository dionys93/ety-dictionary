The Grave Accent in Inglish

In Inglish, the grave accent is used on the following vowels: à, è, ò, ỳ.

The grave accent indicates both pronunciation and stress in the word.

IPA Pronunciations of Accented Vowels:

    Àà: /æ/
    Ỳỳ: /ɪ/
    Èè: /ɛ/
    Òò: /ɔ/

Examples of Words Transcribed from English to Inglish:

    analysis, analytical → anàlysis, analỳtical
    regrets → regrèts
    anemone → anèmonie
    cost → còste
    cough → còffe
    lost → lòst
    Austria → Òstria
