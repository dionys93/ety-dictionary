The Grave Accent in Inglisce

The grave accent is used on the following vowels: à, è, ò.

The grave accent indicates both pronunciation and stress in the word.

IPA Pronunciations of Accented Vowels:

    Àà: /æ/
    Èè: /ɛ/
    Òò: /ɔ/

Examples of Words Transcribed from English to Inglisce:

    analysis → anàlysis
    regrets → regrèts
    anemone → anèmonie
    cost → còste
    cough → còffe
    lost → lòst
    Austria → Òstria
