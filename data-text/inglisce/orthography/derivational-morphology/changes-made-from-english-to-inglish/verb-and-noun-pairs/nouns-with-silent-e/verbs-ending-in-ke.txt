Nouns and Verbs in Inglish: -ke

In Inglish, words that end in -ke follow a consistent pattern for both verb conjugations and noun declensions. 
Verbs ending in -ke retain this ending in most conjugated forms, with the past participle and present participle marked by specific changes. 
The noun forms drop the -ke ending in their plural, replaced with an -ix or -x depending on the root word.

    Noun: bâke, baix
