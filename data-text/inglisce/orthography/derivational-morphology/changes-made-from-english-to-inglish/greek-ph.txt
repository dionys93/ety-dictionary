Modern English ph from Greek φ

F- always begins a word in Inglisce
pharaoh -> fâroa
photo -> foto
phone -> fone

ph if preceding vowel is stressed, otherwise f. 
Suffixes won't mutate established ph
alphabet, elefant, graph, glyph

Examples:
    philosophy: From Greek "φιλοσοφία" (philosophia), meaning "love of wisdom."
    physics: From Greek "φυσική" (physikē), meaning "nature."
    photograph: From Greek "φωτογραφία" (photographia), meaning "drawing with light."

Inglisce:
    filósophie, filosophical
    fisics, fisical
    fôtograph, fôtographic, fotógraphie
    giógraphie, gïographic
