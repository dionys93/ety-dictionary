# The Circumflex ( ̂ )

**Purpose:** Marks special vowel qualities, often diphthongs, historical vowel shifts, or R-colored vowels.

| Grapheme | Phonetic Value | Modern English | Inglisce |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Â** | /eɪ/ | create | **criâte** |
| **Ê** | /ər/ | concern | **concêne** |
| **Î** | /aɪ/ | find | **fînde** |
| **Ô** | /æʊ/ | round <br> sound | **rônd** <br> **sônd** |
| **Ô** | /ʊ/ After u-; Before *-c* | cook, wood, wool | **côc, uôde, uôle** |
| **Û** | /ʊ/ | cushion | **cûcion** |
