# The Tilde ( ̃ )

**Purpose:** Indicates palatalization (the addition of a /j/ glide) or palato-alveolar affrication on specific consonants.

| Grapheme | Phonetic Value | Environment / Usage | Modern English | Inglisce Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **C̃** | /t͡ʃ/ | Always | chin <br> church <br> choice <br> choose <br> cheek <br> cherish | **c̃ine** <br> **c̃urc̃e** <br> **c̃oice** <br> **c̃ouse** <br> **c̃iec** <br> **c̃erișe** |
| **M̃** | /mʲ/ | Palatalized 'm' | museum | **m̃usíom** |
| **Ñ** | /nʲ/ | Palatalized 'n' | convenient | **conviñent** |
