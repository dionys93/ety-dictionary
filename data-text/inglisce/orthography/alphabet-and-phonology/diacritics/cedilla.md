# The Cedilla and Bottom Comma ( ̧ )

**Purpose:** Modifies consonant pronunciation, typically indicating softening or affrication before specific vowels.

| Grapheme | Phonetic Value | Environment / Usage | Modern English | Inglisce Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ç** | /s/ | Before *a, o, u* | juicer <br> publicity | **juçor** <br> **publiçatie** |
| **Ç** | /ʃ/ | Before *i, e* | publish <br> appreciate | **publiçe** <br> **apríçiait** |
| **Ḑ** | /d͡ʒ/ | Affrication | procedure <br> education <br> individual | **procíḑure** <br> **eḑucâcion** <br> **indiviḑual** |
| **Ș** | /ʃ/ | Softening | banish <br> finish <br> tarnish | **banișe** <br> **finișe** <br> **tarnișe** |
| **Ț** | /t͡ʃ/ | Affrication | nature <br> nurture <br> feature <br> fortune <br> fixture <br> mixture <br> creature | **nâţure** <br> **nourţure** <br> **fieţure** <br> **forţon** <br> **fixţure** <br> **mixţure** <br> **crieţure** |
| **Ț** | /ʃ/ | Softening | capture <br> picture | **capţure** <br> **picţure** |
