# The Grave Accent ( ̀ )

**Purpose:** Marks stressed open/lax vowels.

| Grapheme | Phonetic Value | Modern English | Inglisce Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **À** | Stressed /æ/ | analysis | **anàlysis** |
| **È** | Stressed /ɛ/ | anemone | **anèmonie** |
| **Ò** | Stressed /ɔ/ | cost | **còste** |
