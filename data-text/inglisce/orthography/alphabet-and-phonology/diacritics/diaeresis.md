# The Diaeresis ( ¨ )

**Purpose:** Marks **u** in environments where it is difficult to recognize as /w/, and explicitly specifies the pronunciation of **i** / **y** in ambiguous phonetic environments.

| Grapheme | Phonetic Value | Environment / Usage | Modern English | Inglisce Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ü** | /w/ | Prevents misreading of adjacent vowels | award <br> reward | **aüard** <br> **reüard** |
| **Ï / ï** | Stressed /ɪ/ | Before consonants (often in irregular verbs) | bid | **bïde** |
| **Ï / ï** | /i/ | Before vowels (to prevent diphthongization) | nauseate | **nausïait** |
| **Ÿ / ÿ** | Stressed /i/ | | analytical | **analÿtical** |
