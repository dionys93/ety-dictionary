# UY

The digraph **UY** represents a single sound in Inglisce.

---

# I. Pronunciation

| Digraph | Value | Example Sound |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| UY | /aɪ/ | guy |

---

# II. Occurrence

UY is rare in Inglisce and occurs primarily in monosyllabic words.

| Position | Frequency |
|-----------|------------|
| Word-final | Common |

---

# III. Examples

| Modern English | Inglisce | IPA |
|----------------|----------|-----|
| guy, guys | * | /gaɪ/, /gaɪz/ |

---

# IV. Structural Notes

| Pattern | Value |
|----------|--------|
| UY | /aɪ/ |
| UY + plural -S | /aɪz/ |

---

# Teaching Notes

• UY always represents /aɪ/  
• It appears almost exclusively in short, monosyllabic words  
• There are no alternate pronunciations in Inglisce
