-oie verbs drop the -ie and add -ying in their progressive tense.
These verbs include the following:
to annoie (to annoy)
to emploie (to employ)
to enjoie (to enjoy)
to convoie (to convoy)
to deploie (to deploy)
to destroie (to destroy)
