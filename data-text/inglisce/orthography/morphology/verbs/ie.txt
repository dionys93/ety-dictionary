-ie verbs follow the pattern -ies -ied and -ying.

These verbs include the following:
to mârie (to marry)
to bârie (to bury)
to uírie (to weary)
to cârie (to carry)
to copie (to copy)
to studie (to study)
to hêrie (to hurry)
to uêrie (to worry)

Related forms:
mârage (marriage)
bârial (burial)
beiro (barrow)
cârage (carrage)
copie, copis (copy, copies)
studie, studis (study, studies)
student -s
hêrie (hurry)
uêrie, uêris
