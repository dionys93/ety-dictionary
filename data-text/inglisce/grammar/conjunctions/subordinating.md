# Sobórdinaiting Conjonccions

### Concession
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **þo** | Introduces a contrasting statement or concession. | Hie uent autsîde, **þo** it uas raigning. |
| **alþó** | Similar to "þo," introducing a contrasting but true statement. | **Alþó** sie uas tîored, sie caipt uirching. |
| **iven þo** | A stronger form emphasizing the contrast between two clauses. | Ћey lòst þe gâme, **iven þo** þey plaied uell. |
| **uîle** | Indicates a contrast or concession, often meaning "whereas." | **Uîle** I ondrestande your pointe, I disagríe. |

---

### Condicion
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **if** | Introduces a condition that must be met for a result to occur. | Uie'll goe **if** þe ueþre clieres up. |
| **ónly if** | Introduces a strict, exclusive condition. | You may enter **ónly if** you have a tichet. |
| **unless** | Except on the condition that (if not). | Uie'll bie lâte **unless** uie lievfe roiht nôe. |
| **ontill** | Up to the point or event that fulfills a condition. | Don't starte þe teste **ontill** I guive þe signal. |
| **provîded þat** | On the condition or understanding that. | You can bòroe þe carre **provîded þat** you retourne it by nîne. |
| **assouming þat** | Accepting a scenario as true or as a working condition. | **Assouming þat** it dusn't raigne, uie'll have a picnic. |
| **iven if** | Indicates the result will happen regardless of a specific condition. | I'll goe to þe partie **iven if** you don't. |
| **in câss (þat)** | As a precaution against a specific condition or event. | Tâc an ombrella **in câss þat** it raignes. |
| **lest** | With the intention of preventing a condition (for fear that). | Hie spóc quîetly **lest** hie uâc þe bâbie. |

---

### Compaîrasson
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **þan** | Used to connect the second element in a comparison. | Sie's tallar **þan** her broþre. |
| **raþre þan** | Indicates a preference or alternative choice. | I c̃ose tíe **raþre þan** caufie. |
| **uaiþre** | Expresses a doubt or choice between alternatives. | I don't gnoe **uaiþre** to lafhe or crîe. |
| **as moc̃ as** | Compares equal quantities or degrees. | Tâc **as moc̃ as** you nide. |
| **oueras** | In contrast or comparison with the fact that. | Hie loves laûd músic, **oueras** I prefê sîlence. |

---

### Tîme
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **aftre** | In the time following an event. | Uie'll eite **aftre** þe mouvie finișes. |
| **as lòng as** | During the whole time that. | You can staie hiere **as lòng as** you uante. |
| **as soun as** | Immediately at or shortly after the time that. | Call-mi **as soun as** you arrîve. |
| **before** | During the period of time preceding an event. | Uasce your hands **before** dinor. |
| **by þe tîme** | Not later than the moment that an event occurs. | **By þe tîme** uie arrîved, þe seoe had started. |
| **nôe þat** | As a consequence of the fact that (relating to present time). | **Nôe þat** you're hiere, uie can beguin þe mieting. |
| **uance** | As soon as, or from the moment when. | **Uance** you lêne þe reuls, þe gâme's eisy. |
| **since** | In the intervening period between a past time and now. | It's bign raigning **since** morning. |
| **till** | Up to the time of. | Uait **till** I guive þe signal. |
| **ontill** | Up to the point in time or the event mentioned. | Chiep drîving **ontill** you sihe þe signe. |
| **uene** | At or on which time or occasion. | I'll telle'm þe nous **uene** I sihe'm. |
| **uenevre** | At whatever time; on whatever occasion. | Visit-os **uenevre** you're in tône. |
| **uîle** | During the time that; at the same time as. | Ried a bôc **uîle** you uaite. |

### Ríson
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **becase** | For the reason that. | We stayed inside **becase** of the severe storm. |
| **since** | For the reason that (similar to because). | **since** you are here, please help me with this. |
| **so þat** | With the aim that; in order that. | Speak louder **so þat** everyone in the back can hear. |
| **in ordre (þat)** | With the purpose of. | He studied hard **in ordre þat** he might pass the exam. |
| **uoie** | For what reason or purpose. | I don't understand **uoie** she left so early. |

---

### Relatif Adjetif
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **þat** | Used to introduce a defining clause. | The book **þat** you gave me is excellent. |
| **uat** | To what extent, or the thing(s) which. | I know exactly **uat** you mean. |
| **uatevre** | Everything or anything that. | Do **uatevre** you think is best for the team. |
| **uic̃** | Asking for or specifying a choice from a set. | Choose the color **uic̃** you prefer. |
| **uic̃evre** | Any one or more out of a group. | Take **uic̃evre** piece of cake you like. |

---

### Relatif Pronôn
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **hu** | Refers to the person or people performing an action. | The woman **hu** called earlier is my sister. |
| **huevre** | Any person who. | **huevre** finishes the race first wins a prize. |
| **huse** | Showing possession or belonging. | The man **huse** car was stolen reported it to the police. |

---

### Manar
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **hôe** | In what way or manner. | Show me **hôe** this machine works. |
| **as þo** | As would be the case if. | He acted **as þo** he already knew the answer. |
| **as if** | As it would be if. | It looks **as if** it might rain this afternoon. |

---

### Plâce
| Conjonccion | Explanation | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ouere** | In or to what place or position. | This is the town **ouere** I grou up. |
| **ouerevre** | In or to whatever place. | Uie can sitte **ouerevre** you uan'o sitte. |
