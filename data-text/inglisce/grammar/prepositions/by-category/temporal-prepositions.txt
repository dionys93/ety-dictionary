These indicate when someþing happens:

at - at 5 o'clóc, at midnaiht, at Crismass
in - in Jañuairie, in 2024, in þe morning
on - on Monday, on Julîe 4þ
before - before noune
aftre - aftre dinor
during - during þe mieting
for - for tu oars
since - since yesterday
ontill/till - ontill tomòro
by - by next uiec
from...to - from 9 to 5
uiþin - uiþin an oare
þruaut - þruaut þe daie
past - past midnaiht
