These indicate when something happens:

at - at 5 o'clock, at midnight, at Christmas
in - in January, in 2024, in the morning
on - on Monday, on July 4th
before - before noon
after - after dinner
during - during the meeting
for - for two hours
since - since yesterday
until/till - until tomorrow
by - by next week
from...to - from 9 to 5
within - within an hour
throughout - throughout the day
past - past midnight
