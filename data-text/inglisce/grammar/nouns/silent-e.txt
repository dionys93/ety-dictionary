Nouns that are composed of a single circumflex vowel receive a silent -e at the end.

âke
câke
drâke
fâke
mâke
râke
stâke

Unless they end in -c
lâc

Nouns composed of two vowels don't require a silent -e.
pain (noun)
to paine (verb)

Unless the word ends in -l
faile (singular noun and verb form)
