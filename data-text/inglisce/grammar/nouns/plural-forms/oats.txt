Plural Formation for Nouns Ending in -ote and -oate

Nouns ending in -ote and -oate have distinct plural patterns.

1. Words ending in -ote → -oats (come from words that can be verbs or nouns)
   bote → boats
   cote → coats
   note → noats 
   tote → toats
   vote → voats

2. Words ending in -oate → oats (come from words that are only nouns)
   moate → moats
   oate → oats
   goate, goats
