GROUP 2 IRREGULAR VERBS: CONJUGATION PATTERNS

These verbs do not follow the regular "-ed" pattern for past tense formation.


MAJOR CONJUGATION PATTERNS


Pattern 1: Dental Suffix Patterns

Subpattern A: -ende → -ent
bende -s bent -ing
lende -s lent -ing
sende -s sent -ing
spende -s spent -ing

Subpattern B: -iede/-ihe → -ed
bliede -s bled -ing
briede -s bred -ing
fiede -s fed -ing
flihe -s fled -ing
liede -s led -ing
riede -s red -ing

Subpattern C: -ilde → -ilt
builde -s bilt -ing

Subpattern D: -iele → -elt
diele -s delt -ing
fiele -s felt -ing
niele -s nelt -ing

Subpattern E: -olde → -eld
holde -s held -ing
uiþholde -s uiþheld -ing


Pattern 2: Vowel + Consonant Change Patterns

Subpattern A: -iepe → -aipt
quiepe -s caipt -ing
sliepe -s slaipt -ing
suiepe -s suaipt -ing 
uiepe -s uaipt -ing

Subpattern B: -iene/-ievfe → -aint/-aift
miene -s maint -ing
clievfe -s claift -ing
lievfe -s laift -ing

Subpattern C: -iete/-itte → -ait/-at
miete -s mait -ing 
scite -s sciat -ing
sitte: sit(s) sat -ing
spitte: spit(s) spat -ing

Subpattern D: -înde → -âund
bînde -s bâund -ing
fînde -s fâund -ing
grînde -s grâund -ing
uînde -s uâund -ing


Pattern 3: The -oht Pattern
Various verbs forming past with -oht, often with stem changes:

bringue: bring(s) broht -uing
buîe: -s boht byhing
caiche: -s cauht -ing
foîte: -s foht fyhting
ciecue: ciec(s) çoht -uing
tieche: -s tauht -ing
þincue: þinc(s) þoht -uing


Pattern 4: Special Vowel Changes

Subpattern A: -elle → -ôld
telle → tôld
selle → sôld

Subpattern B: -ande → -ôd  
stande -s stôd -ing
ondrestande -s ondrestôd -ing  
uiþstande -s uiþstôd -ing

Subpattern C: Other unique changes
scîne -s sione -ing
sioute -s siat -ing
luîte -s liht -ing
louse -s lost -ing


Pattern 5: Stem-Change Patterns

slîde -s slid -ing


TRANSITIVE VS. INTRANSITIVE DISTINCTIONS

Several verbs in this group show different past forms depending on their grammatical use. This distinction preserves an older feature of English grammar where the verb's role in the sentence affects its form.

hângue: hâng(s) hong -uing (intransitive)
hângue: hâng(s) -ued -uing (transitive)

criepe: -s craipt -ing (intransitive)  
criepe: -s -d -ing (transitive/intransitive)

bêne: -s bênt -ing (intransitive)
bêne: -s -d -ing (transitive)

smelle: -s smelt -ing (intransitive)
smelle: -s -d -ing (transitive)

spuile: -s spuilt spilling (intransitive)
spuile: -s -d spilling (transitive)
