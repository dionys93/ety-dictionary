Regular verbs conjugate in three main combinations. 
For our example, we'll use the verb "to c̃aice" (Modern English: to chase).

1. 3rd Person Singular Present Tense: Sie c̃aices þe cate.
2. Past Tense Form: Sie c̃aiced þe cate. I c̃aiced þe cate. You c̃aiced þe cate.
3. Progressive Tenses: Sie's c̃aicing þe cate. Ћey uere c̃aicing þe cate; etc
