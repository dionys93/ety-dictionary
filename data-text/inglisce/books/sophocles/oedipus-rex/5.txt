PRIESTE

Uate you've said is moast apprópriat,
for þise men hiere have jost informed mi
þat Créon is approc̃ing.


EDIPUS

Lorde Apollo,                                      
as hie retournes, maie fine scîning forțone,
bryht as his côntenance, attende on him.


PRIESTE

It simes þe nous hie brings is gûd—if not,
hie oûd not ueire þat r̃ieþ arônd his hede,
a laurel þichly pached uiþ berris.


EDIPUS

Uie’ll gnoe soun enofh—hie’s uiþin iarsiot.

[Enter CRÉON. EDIPUS calles to him as hie approc̃es.]
