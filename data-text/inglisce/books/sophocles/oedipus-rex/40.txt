[JOCASTA]

Uoie dou you saie þat? [What's] am uêriying you?


[OEDIPUS]

I [thought] I hêd you saie þat [Laius]
is mourdred at [a] plâce ouere þrie roads miete.


[JOCASTA]

[That's] am uate is said and persons [still] believe.


[OEDIPUS]

Ouere am þis plâce? Ouere did it hapane?


[JOCASTA]

In [a] [land] called [Phocis.] Tue roads lide þere—
uan from [Delphi] and uan from [Daulia.]


[OEDIPUS]

Hôe lòng am it since þise evènts tôc plâce?


[JOCASTA]

Ћe [story] is reported in þe citie
jost before you tôc ovre [royal] pôure
hiere in [Thebes.]


[OEDIPUS]

[O] [Zeus,] uate [have] you don?
Uate [have] you planed [for] mie?
