OEDIPUS

[Lady], as I lissane to þise uords [of] yours,
my soele is seacan, my mŷnde [confused] . . .


JOCASTA

Uoie dou you saie þat? Uate’s uêrying you?


OEDIPUS

I þohte I hird you saie þat Laius
uas mourdred at [a] plâce ouere three roads miete.


JOCASTA

Ћat’s uate uas said and pípuls [still] believe.


OEDIPUS

Ouere is þis plâce? Ouere did it hapane?


JOCASTA

In [a] [land] called Phocis. Two roads leade þere—
uan from Delphi and uan from Daulia.


OEDIPUS

Hôe lòng is it since þise evènts tôc plâce?


JOCASTA

Ћe [story] uas reported in þe citie
jost before you tôc ovre [royal] pôure
hiere in Thebes.


OEDIPUS

O Zeus, uate have you don?
Uate have you planed for mi?
