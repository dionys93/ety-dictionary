OEDIPUS

[Lady], as I lissane to þise uords [of] yours,
my soele is seacan, my mŷnde confused . . .


JOCASTA

Uoie dou you saie þat? Uate[’s] uêrying you?


OEDIPUS

I þoht I hêd you saie þat Laius
uas mourdred at [a] plâce ouere three roads miete.


JOCASTA

Ћat[’s] uate uas said and persons [still] believe.


OEDIPUS

Ouere is þis plâce? Ouere did it hapane?


JOCASTA

In [a] [land] called Phocis. Two roads lide þere—
uan from Delphi and uan from Daulia.


OEDIPUS

Hôe lòng is it since þise evènts tôc plâce?


JOCASTA

Ћe [story] uas reported in þe citie
jost before you tôc ovre [royal] pôure
hiere in Thebes.


OEDIPUS

O Zeus, uate have you don?
Uate have you planed for mi?
