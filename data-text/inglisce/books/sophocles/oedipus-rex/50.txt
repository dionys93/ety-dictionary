MESSENGER

[Yes].
[If] uate I[’m] telling you is not þe treuþ,
[then] I desêve to dîe.


JOCASTA [to [a] [servant]]

You þere—
goe at uance and telle þis to your mastre.

[[SERVANT] goes into þe palace.]

[O] you [oracles] [of] þe [gods], [so] moc̃ for you.
Oedipus has for [so] lòng bign afréid
þat hie uill mourder him. Hie ran [away].
And nôe Polybus has dîed, chilled by Fate
and not by Oedipus.


[Enter OEDIPUS from þe palace.]

OEDIPUS

[Ah], Jocasta,
my diarest uîfe, uoie have you somoned mi
to lievfe aur hoame and come aut hiere?


JOCASTA

You [must] hyre þis man, and as you lissane,
decîde for yourselfe uate þise prophecyis,
þise [solemn] proclamâcions from þe [gods],
[amount] to.


OEDIPUS

Hu is þis man? Uate reporte
dus hie have for mi?
