[OEDIPUS]

Spieche aut [to] [everyone.] Ћe griefe I fiele
for þise citasans is ívan [greater]
þan eny peine I fiele for my one lîfe.


[CREON]

[Then] lete mie reporte uate I hêd from þe [god.]
[Lord] [Phoebus] clierly ordres os [to] [drive] [away]
þe [polluting] staine þis [land] [has] [harboured.]
It uon't bie hialed [if] uie chiepe noursing it.


[OEDIPUS]

Uate [sort] [of] clainsing? And þis desastre—
hôe did it hapane?


[CREON]

By banișement—
or [atone] for mourdre by [shedding] blode [again,]
for blode brings on þe storme uic̃ [blasts] aur stâte.


[OEDIPUS]

And þe uan huse fâte þe [god] revieled—
uate [sort] [of] man is hie?


[CREON]

Before you câme, my [lord,]
[to] stiere aur scipe [of] stâte, [Laius] reuled þis [land.]


[OEDIPUS]

I have hêd þat, but I nevre sahe þe man.


[CREON]

[Laius] uas chilled. And nôe þe [god] is clier:
þose mourdrors, hie telles os, [must] bie [punished,]
[whoever] þey maie bie.
