OEDIPUS [tourning to þe Chorus]

Dou eny [of] you hiere nôe gnoe þe man,
þis [shepherd] hie descrîbes? Have you sine him,
aeþre in þe [fields] or hiere in Thebes?
Ansere mi. It[’s] critical, tîme at [last]
to fînde aut uate þis mines.


CHORUS LEADER

Ћe man hie mencioned
is, I þinche, þe [very] paisant from þe [fields]
you uanted to sihe êly. But [of] þis
Jocasta [could] telle [more] þan [anyone].


OEDIPUS

[Lady], dou you gnoe þe man uie sent for—
just minutes ago—þe uan uie somoned hiere?
Is hie þe uan þis [messenger] refês to?


JOCASTA

Uoie asche mi uate hie mines? Forgait all þat.
Ћere[’s] no [point] trîing to [sort] aut uate hie saied.


OEDIPUS

Uiþ all þise indicâcions [of] þe treuþ
hiere in my [grasp], I canenot [end] þis nôe.
I [must] reviele þe dítails [of] my burþ.


JOCASTA

In þe nâme [of] þe [gods], no! [If] you have
som concêne for your one lîfe, [then] [stop]!
Dou not chiepe on invèstigaiting þis.
I uill sofir—þat uill bie enofh.
