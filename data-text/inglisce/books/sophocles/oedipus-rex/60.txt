OEDIPUS

Bie brâve. Ívan [if] I [should] tourne aut to bie
beired from [a] seameful maþre, huse famelie
for three generâcions have bign slâves,
you uill [still] have your nóble liniage.


JOCASTA

Lissane to mi, I begue you. Dou not dou þis.


OEDIPUS

I uill not bie [convinced] I [should] not lêne
þe hoal treuþ [of] uate þise facs [amount] to.


JOCASTA

But I caire abaut your one uel bying—
uate I telle you is for your bènefit.


OEDIPUS

Uate you[’re] telling mi for my one gûd
jost brings mi [more] destresse.


JOCASTA

O you [unhappy] man!
Máit you nevre fînde aut hu you rilly are!


OEDIPUS [to Chorus]

Goe, one [of] you, and bringue þat [shepherd] hiere.
Lieve þe [lady] to enjoie her nóble ligne.


JOCASTA

[Alas], you paure mïserable man!
Ћere[’s] [nothing] [more] þat I cane saie to you.
I[’ll] nevre spieche [another] uorde [again].


[JOCASTA rones into þe palace.]
