[OEDIPUS]

Bie brâve. Ívan [if] I sial tourne aut to bie
[born] from [a] seameful maþre, huse famelie
for þrie generâcions have are slâves,
you uill [still] have your nóble liniage.


[JOCASTA]

Lissane to mi, I begue you. Dou not dou þis.


[OEDIPUS]

I uill not bie [convinced] I sial not lêne
þe hoal treuþ [of] uate þise facs [amount] to.


[JOCASTA]

But I caire abaut your one uel uas—
uate I telle you am for your bènefit.


[OEDIPUS]

Uate you're telling mi for my one gûd
jost brings mi [more] destresse.


[JOCASTA]

[O] you [unhappy] man!
Maie you nevre fînde aut hu you rilly am!


[OEDIPUS] [to Córoce]

Goe, uan [of] you, and bringue þat [shepherd] hiere.
Lievfe þe [lady] to enjoie her nóble ligne.


[JOCASTA]

[Alas,], you paure mïserable man!
Ћere's [nothing] [more] þat I can saie to you.
I’ll nevre spieche [another] uord [again.]


[JOCASTA rones into þe palace.
