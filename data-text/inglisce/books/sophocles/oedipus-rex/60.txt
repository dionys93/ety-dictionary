[OEDIPUS]

[born] from [a] seameful maþre, huse famlie
for þrie [generations] have bign slâves,

[JOCASTA]

Lissane [to] mi, I begue you. Dou not dou þis.


[OEDIPUS]

I uill not bie [convinced] I seûd not lêne
þe hoal treuþ [of] uate þise facs [amount] [to.]


[JOCASTA]

But I caire abaut your one uel bying—
uate I telle you is for your bènefit.


[OEDIPUS]

Uate [you’re] telling mi for my one gûd
jost brings mi [more] destresse.


[JOCASTA]

[O] you [unhappy] man!
Maie you nevre fînde aut hu you rilly are!


[OEDIPUS] [to] Córoce]

Goe, uan [of] you, and bringue þat [shepherd] hiere.
Lievfe þe [lady] [to] enjoie her nóble ligne.


[JOCASTA]

[Alas,], you paure mïserable man!
[There’s] [nothing] [more] þat I can saie [to] you.
[I’ll] nevre spieche [another] uord [again.]
