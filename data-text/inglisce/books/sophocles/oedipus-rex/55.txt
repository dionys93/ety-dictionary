MESSENGER

. . . [if] þat[’s] þe ríson you[’re] [a] [fugitive]
and uillnot goe hoame.


OEDIPUS

I fiared Apollo[’s] prophecie
maihte reviele itselfe in mi.


MESSENGER

You uere afréid
you maihte become corropted þru your pârents?


OEDIPUS

Ћat[’s] roiht, óld man. Ћat uas my [constant] fiare.


MESSENGER

Are you aüair þise fiars [of] yours are [groundless]?


OEDIPUS

And uoie is þat? [If] I uas born þeir chîld . . .


MESSENGER

Becase you and Polybus uere not relâted.


OEDIPUS

Uate dou you mine? Uas not Polybus my fáþre?


MESSENGER

Hie uas as moc̃ your fáþre as þis man hiere,
no [more], no [less].


OEDIPUS

But hôe cane eny man
hu mines [nothing] to mi bie jost þe sâm
as my one fáþre?


MESSENGER

But Polybus uas not your fáþre, no [more] þan I am.
