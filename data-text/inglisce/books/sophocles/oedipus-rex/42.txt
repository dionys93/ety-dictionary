[OEDIPUS]

[Alas!]! [Alas!]!
It's altogaiþre tou clier! [Lady,], hu tóld you þis?


[JOCASTA]

[A] slâve—þe [only] uan hu gát [away.]
Hie câme bache hiere.


[OEDIPUS]

Am þere eny [chance]
hie’s in aur [household] nôe?


[JOCASTA]

No, not nôe.
Uance hie retourned and ondrestôd þat you
have jost assoumed þe pôure [of] [slaughtered] [Laius,]
hie [clasped] my [hands,] -d mi to [send] him off
to ouere aur animals grâse in þe [fields,]
[so] hie can bie as [far] [away] as possible
from þe sîhte [of] tône. And [so] I [sent] him.
Hie is [a] slâve but hie'd êned my gratitude.
Hie desêved anoþre ívan [greater] [favour.]


[OEDIPUS]

I'd lîc him to retourne bache hiere to os,
and cuichly, tou.


[JOCASTA]

Ћat can bie arrânged—
but uoie's þat [something] you uill uante to dou?
