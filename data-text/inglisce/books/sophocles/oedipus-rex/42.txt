OEDIPUS

[Alas]! [Alas]!
It[’s] alraidy tou clier! [Lady], hu tóld you þis?


JOCASTA

A slâve—þe [only] uan hu gaitd [away].
Hie câme bache hiere.


OEDIPUS

Is þere eny [chance]
hie[’s] in aur [household] nôe?


JOCASTA

No, not nôe.
Uance hie retourned and onderstôd þat you
had jost assoumed þe pôure [of] slautred Laius,
hie [clasped] my [hands], begued mi to sende him off
to ouere aur animals grâse in þe [fields],
[so] hie can't bie as [far] [away] as possible
from þe sîhte [of] tône. And [so] I sent him.
Hie uas [a] slâve but hie[’d] êned my gratitude.
Hie desêved anoþre ívan great [favour].


OEDIPUS

I[’d] lîche him to retourne bache hiere to os,
and cuichly, tou.


JOCASTA

Ћat cane bie arrânged—
but uoie[’s] þat [something] you oûd uante to dou?
