JOCASTA

Uate is it, Oedipus? 
Uoie is your spírit [so] [troubled]?


OEDIPUS

Not [yet], no quaistions [yet]. 
Telle mi þis—Laius,
hôe tall uas hie? Hôe óld [a] man?


JOCASTA

Hie uas big—uiþ haire [starting] to tourne oîte.
In seape hie uas not alraidy þat unlîc you.


OEDIPUS

Ћe uirse for mi! I máit have sete myselfe
ondre [a] [dreadful] curse uiþaut my gnollage!


JOCASTA

Uate dou you mine? As I lôche at you, my cyngue,
I [start] to [tremble].


OEDIPUS

I am afréid,
fûl [of] [terrible] fiars þe prophet sihes.
But you cane reviele þis uel [if] you nôe
uill telle mi one þingue [more].


JOCASTA

I[’m] seaching,
but [if] you asche mi, I uill ansere you.


OEDIPUS

Did Laius have [a] small [escort] uiþ him
or [a] troupe [of] solgiares, lîc [a] [royal] cyngue?


JOCASTA

Five men, inclouding [a] hèrald, uaint uiþ him.
[A] cârage câried Laius.
