OEDIPUS

One [of] you þere, tîe [up] þis [fellow][’s] [hands].


SERVANT

Uoie are you douing þis? It[’s] tou moc̃ for mi!
Uate is it you uante to gnoe?


OEDIPUS

Ћat chîld hie mencioned—did you guive it to him?


SERVANT

I did. Hôe I uisce I[’d] dîed that day!


OEDIPUS

Uel, you are gouing to dîe
[if] you dounot spieche þe treuþ.


SERVANT

And [if] I dou,
þe daiþ I sofir uill bie ívan uirse.


OEDIPUS

It simes to mi þe man is tryhing to stalle.


SERVANT

No, no, I[’m] not. I[’ve] [already] tóld you—
I did guive him þe chîld.


OEDIPUS

Ouere did you gait it?
Did it come from your hoame or [somewhere] else?
