MESSENGER

Altogaiþre roiht, [then]. Nôe, telle mi [if] you [recall]
hôe you gâve mi [a] chîld, anoþre [infant] boie,
for mi to reise as my one [foster] sone.


SERVANT

Uate? Uoie asche abaut þat?


MESSENGER

Ћis man hiere, my frende,
uas þat yong chîld bache [then].


SERVANT

Damne you!
Canenot you chiepe quîet abaut it!


OEDIPUS

Hólde on, óld man.
Dounot criticyse him. Uate you have said
is [more] objeccionable þan his accônte.


SERVANT

My nóble mastre, uate have I don ròng?


OEDIPUS

You did not telle os [of] þat [infant] boie,
þe uan hie asched abaut.


SERVANT

Ћat[’s] uate hie sais,
but hie gnoes [nothing]—[a] useless [busybody].


OEDIPUS

[If] you uillnot telle os [of] your one frih uille,
uance uie [start] to hurte you, you uill tauche.


SERVANT

By altogaiþre þe [gods], dounot torțure anoþre óld man!
