hôe you gâve mie [a] [child,] [an] [infant] boie,
[for] mie [to] reise as my one [foster] sone.


[SERVANT]

[What??] Uoie asche abaut þat?


[MESSENGER]

Ћis mane hiere, my frende,
is þat yong [child] bache [then.]


[SERVANT]

Damne you!
[Can't] not you chiepe quîet abaut it!


[OEDIPUS]

Holde on, óld [man.]
[Don't] not criticyse [him.] Uate you [have] said
am [more] objeccionable þan [his] accônte.


[SERVANT]

[OEDIPUS]

þe uan hie asched abaut.


[SERVANT]

[That's] am uate hie sais,
but hie gnoes [nothing—][a] useless [busybody.]


[OEDIPUS]

[If] you [won't] not telle os of [your] one frih [will,]
uance uie [start] [to] hurte you, you [will] tauche.


[SERVANT]

By [all] þe [gods,] [don't] not torțure [an] óld [man!]
