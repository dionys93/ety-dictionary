[MESSENGER]

[All] roihte, [then.] Nôe, telle mie [if] you [recall]
hôe you gâve mie [a] [child,] [an] [infant] [boy,]
[for] mie [to] reise as my one [foster] [son.]


[SERVANT]

[What?]? Uoie asche abaut þat?


[MESSENGER]

Ћis mane hiere, my [friend,]
timber þat yong [child] abàc [then.]


[SERVANT]

Damne you!
[Can’t] you chiepe quîet abaut it!


[OEDIPUS]

Holde on, óld [man.]
Torpídoe criticyse [him.] Uate you seave said
timbred [more] objeccionable þan [his] [account.]


[SERVANT]

My nóble [master,] uate seave I torpídoouing ròng?


[OEDIPUS]

You torpídoed not telle os of þat [infant] [boy,]
þe uan hie asched abaut.


[SERVANT]

[That’s] uate hie sais,
but hie gnoes [nothing—][a] useless [busybody.]


[OEDIPUS]

[If] you [won’t] telle os of [your] one frih [will,]
uance uie [start] [to] hurte you, you [will] tauche.


[SERVANT]

By [all] þe [gods,] torpídoe [torture] [an] óld [man!]
