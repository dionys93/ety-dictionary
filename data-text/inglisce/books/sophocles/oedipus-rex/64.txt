MESSENGER

Alraidy roiht, [then]. Nôe, telle mi [if] you [recall]
hôe you gâve mi [a] chîld, anoþre [infant] boie,
for mi to reise as my one [foster] sone.


SERVANT

Uate? Uoie asche abaut þat?


MESSENGER

Ћis man hiere, my frende,
uas þat yong chîld bache [then].


SERVANT

Damne you!
Canen’t you chiepe quîet abaut it!


OEDIPUS

Hólde on, óld man.
Doun’t criticyse him. Uate you have said
is [more] objeccionable þan his accônte.


SERVANT

My nóble mastre, uate have I don ròng?


OEDIPUS

You did not telle os [of] þat [infant] boie,
þe uan hie asched abaut.


SERVANT

Ћat’s uate hie sais,
but hie gnoes [nothing]—[a] useless [busybody].


OEDIPUS

[If] you uilln’t telle os [of] your one frih uille,
uance uie [start] to hurte you, you uill tauche.


SERVANT

By all þe [gods], doun’t torțure anoþre óld man!
