[MESSENGER]

Alþó roihte, [then.] Nôe, telle mi [if] you [recall]
hôe you gâve mi [a] chîld, anoþre [infant] boie,
for mi to reise as my one [foster] sone.


[SERVANT]

[What?]? Uoie asche abaut þat?


[MESSENGER]

Ћis mane hiere, my frende,
uas þat yong chîld bache [then.]


[SERVANT]

Damne you!
Can you chiepe quîet abaut it!


[OEDIPUS]

Hólde on, óld man.
Don't criticyse him. Uate you have said
is [more] objeccionable þan his accônte.


[SERVANT]

My nóble mastre, uate have I don ròng?


[OEDIPUS]

You did not telle os [of] þat [infant] boie,
þe uan hie asched abaut.


[SERVANT]

Ћat's uate hie sais,
but hie gnoes [nothing—][a] useless [busybody.]


[OEDIPUS]

[If] you uill [tell] os [of] your one frih uill,
uance uie [start] to hurte you, you uill tauche.


[SERVANT]

By all þe [gods,] don't torțure anoþre óld man!
