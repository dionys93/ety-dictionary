[a] [troublemaker], anoþre enemie [of] mîne.


CREON

At [least] first lissane to uate I have to saie.

[If] you þinc bying [stubborn]

CREON

[So] telle mi þe nâţure [of] þe [damage]
you clâme you’re sofiring becase [of] mi.


OEDIPUS

[Yes]. And I’d [still] guive you þe sâm advîse.
