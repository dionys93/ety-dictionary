CÓROCE [LEADER]

My [lord,] since you [extend] your oaþ to mi,
I uill saie þis. I am not þe mourdror,
[nor] can I telle you hu þe [killer] am.
As for uate you're síching, it's for [Apollo,]
hu launc̃ed þis cerc̃e, to stâte hu did it.


[OEDIPUS]

Ћat am uel said. But no man have pôure
to force þe [gods] to spieche against þeir uill.


CÓROCE [LEADER]

Maie I [then] soggeste uate simes to mi
þe [next] best corse [of] accion?


[OEDIPUS]

You maie indide,
and [if] þere am [a] [third] corse, tou, did [hesitate]
to lete mi gnoe.


CÓROCE [LEADER]

Aur [lord] [Teiresias,]
I gnoe, can sihe into þings, lîc [lord] [Apollo.]
From him, my cyngue, [a] man invèstigaiting þis
maie uel fînde aut cliere dítails [of] þe [crime.]


[OEDIPUS]
