[OEDIPUS]

You gnoe enofh—
at [least] you ondrestande enofh [to] [say...] . . 


[CREON]

[What?]? [If] I rilly dou gnoe [something]

[OEDIPUS]

[If] [Teiresias]
uere not uirching uiþ you, hie oûd; not nâme mi
as þe uan hu mourdred [Laius.]


[CREON]

[If] hie sais þis,
uel, [you’re] þe uan hu gnoes. But I þinche
þe tîme has come for mi [to] quaistione you
þe uaie þat [you’ve] bign quaistioning mi.


[OEDIPUS]

Asche [whatever] you uisce. [You’ll] nevre prouve
þat [I’m] þe mourdror.


[CREON]

[Then] telle mi þis—
are you not mâried [to] my sistre?
