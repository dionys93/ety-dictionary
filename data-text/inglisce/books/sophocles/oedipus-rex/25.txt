But hu am þe [man,] þe uan
hu uiþ [his] blode-red [hands]
[has] don [unspeakable] brutaletie?
Ћe tîme [has] come [for] [him] [to] flihe—
[to] mouve [his] [powerful] [foot]
[more] [swiftly] þan þose houves

Against [him] [Zeus's] sone nôe springs,
armed uiþ loihtning fŷre and liding on
þe [inexorable] and terrifafyhing [Furies.]

[to] síche þe uan [whom] no uan gnoes.

hidan in þe [untamed] uôde,
þru rox and câves, [alone]
uiþ [his] despeire on [joyless] fûte,

[uttered] at [earth's] [central] nâvele stone.
