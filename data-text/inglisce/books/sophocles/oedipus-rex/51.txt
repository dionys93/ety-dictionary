[JOCASTA]

Hie comes from [Corinth,]
bringuing nouly þat [Polybus,] your fáþre,
no [longer] is alîve. Hie’s daid.


[OEDIPUS]

[What?]?
Strângeur, lete mi hyre from you in person.


[MESSENGER]

[If] I [must] [first] reporte my nouly quît [plainly,]
[then] I seûd lete you gnoe þat [Polybus]
has passed [away.] Hie’s gaon.
 

[OEDIPUS]

By traic̃erie,
or uas it þe [result] [of] som desise?


[MESSENGER]

Uiþ óld bodie [a] [slight] ueihte on þe [scales]
brings fînal pice.


[OEDIPUS]

Apperently his daiþ
uas from anoþre [illness?]


[MESSENGER]

[Yes,], and from óld âge.
