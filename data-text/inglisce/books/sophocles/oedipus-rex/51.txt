JOCASTA

Hie comes from Corinth,
bringuing nous þat Polybus, your fáþre,
no lòng is alîve. Hie’s daid.


OEDIPUS

Uate?
Strângeur, lete mi hyre from you in person.


MESSENGER

[If] I [must] first reporte my nous quît [plainly],
[then] I seûd lete you gnoe þat Polybus
has past [away]. Hie’s gaon.
 

OEDIPUS

By traic̃erie,
or uas it þe [result] [of] som desise?


MESSENGER

Uiþ óld bodie [a] [slight] ueihte on þe [scales]
brings fînal pice.


OEDIPUS

[Apparently] his daiþ
uas from anoþre [illness]?


MESSENGER

[Yes], and from óld âge.
