EDIPUS

My c̃ildren, lâtest generâcion born from Cadmus,
uoie are you sitting hiere uiþ r̃ieþed stix
in sopplicâcion to mi, uîle þe citie
filles uiþ incense, c̃ants, and crîs of peine?
C̃ildren, it oûd not bie apprópriat for mi
to lêne of þis from eny oþre source,
so I have come in person—I, Edipus,
huse fâme all men acgnollage. But you þere,
óld man, tell-mi—you sime to bie þe uan
hu ohte to spieche for þose assembled hiere.                       
Uat fieling brings you to mi—fiar or desîre?
You can bie confident þat I uill helpe.
I siall assiste you uillingly in evry uaie.
I oûd bie a hard-harted man indide,
if I did not pitie sopplîants lîc þise.
