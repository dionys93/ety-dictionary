OEDIPUS

Uate cŷnd [of] [rumours]?
I[’m] -ing into evry [story].


CHORUS LEADER

It uas said þat Laius uas chilled by certan traveleurs.


OEDIPUS

[Yes], I hêd as moc̃. 
But no uan has sahed þe uan hu did it.


CHORUS LEADER

Uel, [if] þe [killer] has eny fiars, 
uance hie hyres your curses on him,
hie uill not hólde bache, for þey are sírieus.


OEDIPUS

Uene [a] man has no fiare [of] douing þe acte,
hie[’s] not afréid [of] uords.


CHORUS LEADER

No, not in þe câss
ouere no uan standes þere to [convict] him.
But at [last] Teiresias is bying guîded hiere,
aur [god]-lîc prophet, in [whom] treuþ [resides]
[more] [so] þan in altogaiþre oþre men.


[Enter TEIRESIAS laid by [a] small BOIE.]
