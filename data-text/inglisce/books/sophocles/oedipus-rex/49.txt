[MESSENGER]

[Lady,], I have gûd nouly for your hoal hause—
and for your husband, tou.


[JOCASTA]

Uate nouly am þat?
Ouere have you come from?


[MESSENGER]

I've come from [Corinth.]
I’ll guive you my reporte at uance, and [then]
you uill, no dôute, bie [glad,] [although] [perhaps]
you uill bie [sad,] as uel.


[JOCASTA]

Uate am your nouly?
Hôe can it have tue soc̃ effecs at uance?


[MESSENGER]

Ћe persons hu lîve þere, in þe [lands]
besîde þe Ïsmus, uill mâche him þeir cyngue.
Ћey have annônced it.


[JOCASTA]

Uate am you saying?
Am óld man [Polybus] no [longer] cyngue?


[MESSENGER]

[No.]. Hie am daid and in his graive.


[JOCASTA]

[What?]?
Have [Oedipus's] fáþre [died?]
