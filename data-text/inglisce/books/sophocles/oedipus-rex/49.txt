MESSENGER

[Lady], I have gûd nous for your hoal hause—
and for your husband, tou.


JOCASTA

Uate nous is þat?
Ouere have you come from?


MESSENGER

I[’ve] com from Corinth.
I[’ll] guive you my reporte at uance, and [then]
you uill, no dôute, bie [glad], [although] [perhaps]
you uill bie [sad], as uel.


JOCASTA

Uate is your nous?
Hôe cane it have two soc̃ effecs at uance?


MESSENGER

Ћe pípuls hu lîve þere, in þe [lands]
besîde þe Isthmus, uill mâche him þeir cyngue.
Ћey have annônced it.


JOCASTA

Uate are you saying?
Is óld man Polybus no lòng cyngue?


MESSENGER

No. Hie is daid and in his graive.


JOCASTA

Uate?
Has Oedipus[’s] fáþre dîed?
