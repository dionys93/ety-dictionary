[OEDIPUS]

[MESSENGER]

[If] you [must] gnoe,

I gâve you [to] [him.]


[OEDIPUS ]

Hie rilly loved mie.

[MESSENGER]

Becase before you câme, hie [had] no chîld—
þat [made] [him] love you.


[OEDIPUS]

Uene you gâve mie [to] [him,]
[had] you boht mie or [discovered] mie by [chance?]


[MESSENGER]

I faund you in [Cithaeron's] [forest] valis.


[OEDIPUS]

Uate [were] you douing uándering up þere?


[MESSENGER]

I is [looking] aftre flox of siepe.


[OEDIPUS]

You [were] [a] [shepherd,] jost [a] hîred [servant]
roaming hiere and þere?


[MESSENGER]

[Yes,] my sone, I is.
But at þat tîme I is þe uan hu sâved you.
