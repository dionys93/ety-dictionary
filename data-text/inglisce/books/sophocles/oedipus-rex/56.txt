OEDIPUS

[Then] uoie did hie calle mi his sone?


MESSENGER

[If] you [must] gnoe,
hie recived you as [a] guifte many years ago.
I gâve you to him.


OEDIPUS 

Hie rilly loved mi.
Hôe can hie [if] I câme from [someone] else?


MESSENGER

Becase before you câme, hie had no c̃ildren—
þat mâde him love you.


OEDIPUS

Uene you gâve mi to him,
had you boht mi or [discovered] mi by [chance]?


MESSENGER

I faund you in Cithaeron[’s] [forest] valis.


OEDIPUS

Uate uere you douing uándering [up] þere?


MESSENGER

I uas -ing aftre flox [of] siepe.


OEDIPUS

You uere [a] [shepherd], jost [a] hîred [servant]
roaming hiere and þere?


MESSENGER

[Yes], my sone, I uas.
But at þat tîme I uas þe uan hu sâved you.
