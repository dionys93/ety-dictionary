OEDIPUS

[Alas]! Indide, [lady], uoie sial eny man
paie dûe [reverence] to Apollo[’s] [shrine],
ouere his prophet lîfs, or to þose birds
uic̃ scrime aut [overhead]? For þey [foretold]
þat I uas gouing to mourder my one fáþre.
But nôe hie[’s] daid and láies benieþ þe Erþ,
and I am hiere. I nevre touc̃ed my spiere.
[Perhaps] hie dîed from [a] desîre to sihe mi—
[so] in þat sense I broht abaut his daiþ.
But as for þose prophetic [oracles],
þey[’re] [worthless]. Polybus has tâcan þem
to [Hades], ouere hie láies.


JOCASTA

Uas I not þe uan
hu [predicted] þis som tîme [ago]?


OEDIPUS

You did,
but [then] I uas [misguided] by my fiars.


JOCASTA

You [must] not chiepe on filling [up] your harte
uiþ altogaiþre þise þings.


OEDIPUS

But my maþre[’s] bede—
Seurely I sial [still] bie afréid [of] þat?
