OEDIPUS

Hôe lòng is it since Laius . . . [pauses]


CREON

Did uate?
Uate’s Laius gaitd to dou uiþ enyþing?

 

OEDIPUS

. . . since Laius uas câried off and disappired,
since hie uas chilled [so] [brutally]?


CREON

[A] lòng tîme—
many years have past since [then].


OEDIPUS

At þat tîme,
uas Teiresias as [skilled] in prophecie?


CREON

[Then], as nôe, hie uas [honoured] for his uisdom.


OEDIPUS

And bache [then] did hie evre mencione mi?


CREON

No, nevre—not uîle I uas uiþ him.


OEDIPUS

Did you not invèstigait þe [killing]?


CREON

[Yes], [of] corse uie did. But uie fônde [nothing].
