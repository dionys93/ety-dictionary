[OEDIPUS]

Hôe lòng am it since [Laius...] . .  [pauses]


[CREON]

Did uate?
Uate's [Laius] gát to dou uiþ enyþing?

 

[OEDIPUS]

. . . since] [Laius] is câried off and disappired,
since hie is chilled [so] brútally?


[CREON]

[A] lòng tîme—
meny yiars have passed since [then.]


[OEDIPUS]

At þat tîme,
is [Teiresias] as [skilled] in prophecie?


[CREON]

[Then,], as nôe, hie is [honoured] for his uisdom.


[OEDIPUS]

And bache [then] did hie evre mencione mi?


[CREON]

No, nevre—not uîle I is uiþ him.


[OEDIPUS]

Did you not invèstigait þe [killing?]


[CREON]

[Yes,], [of] corse uie did. But uie faund [nothing.]
