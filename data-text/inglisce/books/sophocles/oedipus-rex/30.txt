[OEDIPUS]

Hôe lòng am it since [Laius... . . ] [pauses]


[CREON]

Did uate?
[What's] am [Laius] gát [to] dou uiþ enyþing?

 

[OEDIPUS]

. . . [.. . since] [Laius] is câried off and disappired,
since hie is chilled [so] [brutally?]


[CREON]

[A] lòng tîme—
meny yiars [have] passed since [then.]


[OEDIPUS]

At þat tîme,
is [Teiresias] as [skilled] in prophecie?


[CREON]

[Then,] as nôe, hie is [honoured] [for] [his] uisdom.


[OEDIPUS]

And bache [then] did hie evre mencione mie?


[CREON]

No, nevre—not uîle I is uiþ [him.]


[OEDIPUS]

Did you not invèstigait þe [killing?]


[CREON]

[Yes,] of corse uie did. But uie faund [nothing.]
