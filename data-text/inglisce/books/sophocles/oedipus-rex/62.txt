You [elders], þó I’ve nevre sine þe man
uie’ve bign síching for [a] lòng tîme nôe,
[if] I had to guesse, I þinc I sihe him.
Hie’s coming hiere. Hie lôcs [very] óld—

And I gnoe þe pípuls coming uiþ him,
[servants] [of] mîne. But [if] you’ve sine him before,

CHORUS LEADER

[Yes], I recognyse þe man. Ћere’s no dôute.
Hie uirched for Laius—[a] [trusty] [shepherd].

[Enter SERVANT, anoþre óld [shepherd].]


OEDIPUS

Stranger from Corinth, lete mi first asche you—
is þis þe man you spóc [of]?


MESSENGER

[Yes], hie is—
hie’s þe man you sihe in fronte [of] you.


OEDIPUS

You, óld man, ovre hiere. Lôche at mi.
Nôe ansere uate I asc. Som tîme [ago]
did you uirche for Laius?


SERVANT

[Yes], as [a] slâve.
But I uas not boht. I grou [up] in his hause.
