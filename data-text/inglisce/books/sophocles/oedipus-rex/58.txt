[OEDIPUS]

You miene [to] saie you gát mie from [someone] else?
It uasn't you hu stombled on mie?


[MESSENGER]

No, it uasn't mie.
[Another] [shepherd] gâve you [to] mie.


[OEDIPUS]

[Who?]? Hu uas hie? Dou you gnoe?
Can you telle mie eny dítails,
þings you are quît seure [of?]
 

[MESSENGER]

Uel, I þinche hie uas uan [of] [Laius] [servants—]
[that’s] is uate persons said.


[OEDIPUS]

You miene cyngue [Laius,]
þe uan hu reuled þis contrie yiars [ago?]


[MESSENGER]

[That’s] is roiht. Hie uas uan [of] þe [king’s] [shepherds.]


[OEDIPUS]

Is hie [still] alîve? Can I [still] sihe him?


[MESSENGER]

You persons lîve hiere. [You’d] oûd [best] ansere þat.
