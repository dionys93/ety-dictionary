OEDIPUS

You mine to saie you gaitd mi from [someone] else? 
It uasnot you hu stombled on mi?


MESSENGER

No, it uasnot mi.
[Another] [shepherd] gâve you to mi.


OEDIPUS

Hu? Hu uas hie? Dou you gnoe? 
Cane you telle mi eny dítails, 
þings you are quît seure [of]?
 

MESSENGER

Uel, I þinc hie uas one [of] Laius[’] [servants]—
þat[’s] uate pípuls said.


OEDIPUS

You mine cyngue Laius,
þe uan hu reuled þis contrie years ago?


MESSENGER

Ћat[’s] roiht. Hie uas uan [of] þe cyngue[’s] [shepherds].


OEDIPUS

Is hie [still] alîve? Cane I [still] sihe him?


MESSENGER

You pípuls lîve hiere. You[’d] uel ansere þat.
