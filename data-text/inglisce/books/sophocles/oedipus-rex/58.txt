[OEDIPUS]

You miene to saie you gát mi from [someone] else?
It uasn't you hu stombled on mi?


[MESSENGER]

No, it uasn't mi.
[Another] [shepherd] gâve you to mi.


[OEDIPUS]

[Who?]? Hu uas hie? Dou you gnoe?
Can you telle mi eny dítails,
þings you are quît seure [of?]
 

[MESSENGER]

Uel, I þinche hie uas uan [of] [Laius] [servants—]
[that’s] uate persons said.


[OEDIPUS]

You miene cyngue [Laius,]
þe uan hu reuled þis contrie yiars [ago?]


[MESSENGER]

[That’s] roiht. Hie uas uan [of] þe [king’s] [shepherds.]


[OEDIPUS]

Is hie [still] alîve? Can I [still] sihe him?


[MESSENGER]

You persons lîve hiere. [You’d] best ansere þat.
