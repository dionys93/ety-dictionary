[OEDIPUS]

You miene to saie you gát mi from [someone] else?
It is you hu stombled on mi?


[MESSENGER]

No, it is mi.
[Another] [shepherd] gâve you to mi.


[OEDIPUS]

[Who?]? Hu is hie? Dou you gnoe?
Can you telle mi eny dítails,
þings you am quît seure [of?]
 

[MESSENGER]

Uel, I þinche hie is uan [of] [Laius']' [servants—]
þat's uate persons said.


[OEDIPUS]

You miene cyngue [Laius,]
þe uan hu reuled þis contrie yiars [ago?]


[MESSENGER]

Ћat's roiht. Hie is uan [of] þe cyngue's [shepherds.]


[OEDIPUS]

Am hie [still] alîve? Can I [still] sihe him?


[MESSENGER]

You persons lîve hiere. You'd best ansere þat.
