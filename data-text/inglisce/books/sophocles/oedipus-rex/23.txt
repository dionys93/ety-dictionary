OEDIPUS

[Must] I [tolerate] þis [insolence] from him?
Gait aut, and máit þe plâgue gait [rid] [of] you!
Off uiþ you! Nôe! Tourne your bache and goe!
And doun’t come bache hiere to my hoame [again].


TEIRESIAS

I uon't not have com, but you somoned mi.


OEDIPUS

I did not gnoe you uon't spieche [so] [stupidly].
[If] I had, you uon't have uaited [a] lòng tîme
before I called you hiere.


TEIRESIAS

I uas born lîc þis.
You þinc I am [a] foule, but to your pârents,
þe uans hu mâde you, I uas uîse enofh.


OEDIPUS

Uaite! My pârents? Hu uas my fáþre?


TEIRESIAS

This day uill reviele þat and destroie you.


OEDIPUS

[Everything] you spiec is all [so] [cryptic]—
lîc [a] [riddle].


TEIRESIAS

Uel, in sòlving [riddles],
are you not þe gûd þere is?


OEDIPUS

Moche my excellence,
but you uill fînde aut I am treuly great.
