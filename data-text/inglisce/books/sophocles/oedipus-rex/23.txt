[OEDIPUS]

[Must] I [tolerate] þis [insolence] from him?
Gait aut, and maie þe plâgue gait [rid] [of] you!
Off uiþ you! [Now!]! Tourne your bache and goe!
And don't come bache hiere to my hoame [again.]


[TEIRESIAS]

I uill not have come, but you somoned mi.


[OEDIPUS]

I did not gnoe you uill spieche [so] [stupidly.]
[If] I had, you uill have uaited [a] lòng tîme
before I called you hiere.


[TEIRESIAS]

I uas [born] lîc þis.
You þinche I am [a] foule, but to your pârents,
þe [ones] hu mâde you, I uas uîse enofh.


[OEDIPUS]

[Wait!]! My pârents? Hu uas my fáþre?


[TEIRESIAS]

Ћis daie uill reviele þat and [destroy] you.


[OEDIPUS]

[Everything] you spieche is all [so] [cryptic—]
lîc [a] [riddle.]


[TEIRESIAS]

Uel, in sòlving [riddles,]
are you not þe best þere is?


[OEDIPUS]

Moche my excellence,
but you uill fînde aut I am treuly great.
