OEDIPUS

I [must] [obey],
[although] þat’s not uate I desîre.


CREON

In dûe tîme all þings uill uirche aut for þe gûd.


OEDIPUS

I uill goe.
But you gnoe þere are condicions.


CREON

Telle mi.
Uance I hyre þem, I’ll gnoe uate þey are.


OEDIPUS

Sende mi [away] to lîve autsîde [of] Thebes.


CREON

[Only] þe [god] cane guive you uate you asc.


OEDIPUS

But I’ve becom abhorent to þe [gods].


CREON

[Then] you seûd cuichly gait uate you desîre.


OEDIPUS

[So] you agríe?


CREON

I doun’t lîche to spieche [thoughtlessly] and saie uate I doun’t mine.


OEDIPUS

Come [then], leade mi off.
