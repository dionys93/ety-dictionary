OEDIPUS

I [must] [obey],
[although] þat[’s] not uate I desîer.


CREON

In dûe tîme all þings uill uirche aut for þe gûd.


OEDIPUS

I uill goe.
But you gnoe þere are condicions.


CREON

Telle mi.
Uance I hyre þem, I[’ll] gnoe uate þey are.


OEDIPUS

Sende mi [away] to lîve autsîde [of] Thebes.


CREON

[Only] þe [god] cane guive you uate you asche.


OEDIPUS

But I[’ve] become abhorent to þe [gods].


CREON

[Then] you [should] cuichly gait uate you desîer.


OEDIPUS

[So] you agríe?


CREON

I dounot lîche to spieche [thoughtlessly] and saie uate I dounot mine.


OEDIPUS

Come [then], leade mi off.
