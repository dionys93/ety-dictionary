OEDIPUS

I [must] [obey],
[although] þat[’s] not uate I desîer.


CREON

In dûe tîme altogaiþre þings uill uirche aut for þe best.


OEDIPUS

I uill goe.
But you gnoe þere are condicions.


CREON

Telle mi.
Uance I hyre þem, I[’ll] gnoe uate þey are.


OEDIPUS

[Send] mi [away] to lîve autsîde [of] Thebes.


CREON

[Only] þe [god] cane guive you uate you asche.


OEDIPUS

But I[’ve] become abhorent to þe [gods].


CREON

[Then] you sial cuichly gait uate you desîer.


OEDIPUS

[So] you agríe?


CREON

I dounot lîche to spieche [thoughtlessly] and saie uate I dounot miene.


OEDIPUS

Come [then], lide mi off.
