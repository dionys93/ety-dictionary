Inglisce Lânguage Spelling Reforme

Meny propôsals have bign mâde to chânge þe sistem of Inglisce orþógraphie uiþ þi eime of mâcuing it more consistent and clôssar to þe spocan lânguage. 
Cómon môtifs for spelling reforme include mâcuing lêning cuicar and chiepar, þereby mâcuing Inglisce more useful as an intrenacional lânguage.

Reforme propôsals vârie uîdly in þe scope and depþ of þeir chânges. Uîle som eime to unifôrmly folloe þi alphabetic principle (occâsionally by criâting nou alphabets),
oþers mierly suggeste chânging a feu cómon uords. Consêvatif propôsals trîe to improuve þi existing sistem by úsing þe tradicional Inglisce alphabet, 
mainteining þe familliar seaps of uords and aplyhing existing convencions more regularly (soch as sîlent e). 
More radical propôsals máit complitely restrucțure þe lôc and fiele of þe sistem. Som reformeurs prefê a grad̦ual chânge implemented in staiges, uîle oþers fâvore an 
immídiat and tôtal reforme for all.

Som spelling reforme propôsals have bign adopted parcially or temporârily. Meny of þe spellings prefêd by Noah Webster have becom standard in þe Unîted Stâts, 
but have not bign adopted elsouere (sihe Amèrican and Britisce Inglisce spelling diffrences).
