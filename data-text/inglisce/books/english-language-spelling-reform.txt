Inglisce Lânguage Spelling Reforme

Meny propósals have bign mâde to c̃ainge þe sistem of Inglisce orþographie uiþ þi eime of mâching it more consistent and clóssar to þe spocan lânguage. 
Common mótifs for spelling reforme incloude mâching lêning cuicar and c̃iepar, þereby mâching Inglisce more useful as an intrenacional lânguage.

Reforme propósals vârie uîdly in þe scope and depþ of þeir c̃ainges. Uîle som éime to unifórmly folloe þi alphabetic principle (occâsionally by criâting nou alphabets),
oþars mierly suggeste c̃ainging a feu common uords. Consêvatif propósals trîe to improuve þi existing sistem by using þe tradicional Inglisce alphabet, 
mainteining þe familliar seaps of uords and applyhing existing convencions more regularly (soc̃ as sîlent e). 
More radical propósals máit complitely restrucțure þe lôc and fiele of þe sistem. Som reformeurs prefê a grad̦ual c̃ainge implemented in staiges, uîle oþars fâvore an immídiat and tótal reforme for all.

Som spelling reforme propósals have bign adopted parcially or temporârily. Meny of þe spellings prefêd by Noah Webster have becom standard in þe Unîted Stâts, 
but have not bign adopted elsouere (sihe Amèrican and Britisce Inglisce spelling diffrences).
