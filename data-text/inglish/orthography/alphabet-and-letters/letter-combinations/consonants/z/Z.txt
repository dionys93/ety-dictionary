Z

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Z represents one sound:
• /z/ — as in "zoo"

OCCURRENCE
Less common than S for the /z/ sound.

EXAMPLES
    zoo → zou
    zoology → zuólogie
    frieze → friez
    Chinese → Chîníz
    Portuguese → Porȶuguíz

TEACHING NOTES
Z = /z/ always. Often appears in words of foreign origin.
