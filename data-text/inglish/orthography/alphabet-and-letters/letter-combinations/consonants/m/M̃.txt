M̃

PRONUNCIATION
The letter M̃ represents one sound:
• /mj/ — M followed by a yod

EXAMPLES
    ammunition → am̃onicion
    to amuse → ta am̃use
    museum → m̃usíom
    music → m̃usic
    amulet → am̃olet

TEACHING NOTES
M̃ = /mj/ (M + yod). Used where Modern English has "mu" pronounced with a /j/ sound.
