Í

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Í represents one sound:
• /i/ — as in "machine" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Í marks stressed /i/ in words.

EXAMPLES
    completion → complícion
    deletion → delícion
    sieve → síve
    conceive → concíve
    conceit → concípt
    receive → recíve
    receipt → recípt
    deceive → decíve
    deceit → decípt
    immediate → immídiat
    media → mídia
    idea → îdía

TEACHING NOTES
Í = stressed /i/. Often appears in Latin-derived words and where English has "ei" = /i/.
