Ú

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ú represents one sound:
• /u/ — as in "moon" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ú shows where the stress lies in the word.

EXAMPLES
    kangaroo(s) → kângarue, kângarús
    
    shampoo(s) → shampue, shampús
    
    root → rute
    roots → rúts
    
    suit → sute
    suits → súts
    
    recruit → recrute
    recruits → recrúts

TEACHING NOTES
Ú = stressed /u/. Note how it often appears in plural forms.
