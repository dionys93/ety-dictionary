Ò

PRONUNCIATION
The letter Ò represents one sound:
• /ɔ/ — as in "caught" (always stressed)

PURPOSE
Ò marks stressed /ɔ/ in words where stress placement needs clarification.

EXAMPLES
    cost → còst
    frost → fròst
    cough → còff

TEACHING NOTES
Ò = stressed /ɔ/. Used to show both pronunciation and stress.
