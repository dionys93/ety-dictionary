AÊ

PRONUNCIATION
The digraph AÊ represents one sound:
• /aɪər/ — as in "iron"

OCCURRENCE
This letter combination is rare in Inglish.

EXAMPLES
    iron → aên /aɪərn/
    quire → quaê /kwaɪər/

TEACHING NOTES
AÊ is one of the rarest digraphs in Inglish. It represents the /aɪər/ sound in a small number of words.
