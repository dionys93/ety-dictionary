Nouns and Verbs in Inglish: Double S

In Inglish, nouns ending in double -s drop the -e when they are transformed into verb forms. 
The plural form of these nouns is -(e)s.

Examples:

    to lísse → þe líss(es) /liːs/

    to relísse → þe relíss(es) /rɪˈliːs/

    to grísse → þe gríss /ɡriːs/

    to crísse → þe críss(es) /kriːs/
